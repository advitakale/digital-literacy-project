# Stay Safe Online — Prevention Checklist
### For College Students in India

1. Never click on suspicious links received via
   email, SMS, or WhatsApp — always verify the
   source first.

2. Always check the website URL before entering
   any login or personal details — look for
   https:// and the correct domain name.

3. Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank password
   with anyone — not even someone claiming to be
   from your bank. (UPI Safety)

4. Never save your UPI PIN or net banking password
   on your phone or in chat messages. Change them
   regularly. (UPI Safety)

5. Use strong and unique passwords for every
   account — include letters, numbers, and symbols.

6. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all
   important accounts like email, LinkedIn, and
   GitHub.

7. Do not connect to unknown or public Wi-Fi
   networks when accessing banking or college
   portals.

8. Regularly update your phone, apps, and browser
   to protect against security vulnerabilities.

9. Do not accept unknown friend requests or respond
   to strangers asking for personal information
   online.

10. If you receive a suspicious call or message
    asking for money or personal details, hang up
    and verify independently.
