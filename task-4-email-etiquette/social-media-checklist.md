# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## Do's
- Do keep your profiles professional and up to date
- Do use privacy settings to control who sees your posts
- Do think before you post — ask if it reflects you well
- Do give credit when sharing someone else's content
- Do use social media to network and learn new things

## Don'ts
- Don't share personal information like address or phone number
- Don't post anything offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate
- Don't share OTPs, passwords, or financial details online
- Don't engage with or spread unverified news or rumours
- Don't accept friend/follow requests from unknown strangers
